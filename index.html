Apple Picking Season Now Open!

Each September and October, we welcome the public to our Farm for apple picking in our beautiful, hillside orchard! Guests can enjoy a classic Fall day, strolling the grounds while taking in the breathtaking, panoramic views of the beautiful Hudson Valley.

We offer something for guests of all ages: BBQ lunch, hot apple cider donuts, refreshing apple cider slushies, fresh baked pies, pumpkins, and gifts, all while enjoying live country music. For our adult guests, bartenders will be slinging our locally famous Hardscrabble Cider produced right here on our Farm.?
Join us for a truly authentic day out in the country in the beautiful Hudson Valley!
